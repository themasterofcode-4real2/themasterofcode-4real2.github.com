Why CustomHits.net is a Waste of Time!

Posted on March 31, 2025 at 9:45 PM EST

CustomHits.net was a complete waste of time for me. I was hoping to get some solid results, but instead, I got a bunch of headaches and delays.

First off, it didn’t even work for my YouTube Shorts. No views, no engagement—just nothing. I waited and waited, but my video counter stayed the same.

Then I decided to test if their system was actually doing anything. I used a URL shortener to track the traffic, and guess what? None of the views registered until my request was "sent for manual review." So basically, they hold back your views and only process them when they feel like it.

The waiting times are ridiculous. You don’t just start getting views when you submit a link. You have to wait, hope, and then maybe—if you're lucky—they’ll approve it.

The interface looks outdated and clunky. It feels like a site that hasn’t been updated in years, and navigating it is more frustrating than anything else.

Another huge issue is the "credits" system. It’s set up in a way that makes earning anything worthwhile a struggle, pushing you to buy credits instead. But if the free views don’t work right, why would I pay for them?

Support is a joke. I reached out for help, and either they took forever to respond or they just ignored my questions altogether.

To top it all off, the traffic quality is questionable. Even when I did get views, I didn’t notice any real engagement or retention. It felt like fake traffic, not real viewers.

Bottom line: CustomHits.net isn’t worth your time. If you’re trying to grow your YouTube channel, there are way better options out there. Avoid the frustration and look elsewhere.
